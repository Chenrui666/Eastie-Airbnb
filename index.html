Is Airbnb taking over the East Boston?

Airbnb is a website allowing owner renting thier rooms to others for a few nights. There are 4,870 listings rooms in Boston. 259 of them are located in East Boston whose average price is much higher than the normal rental house. Besides, 60.2% of these rooms are highly available (more than 60 days per year) year-round for tourists, probably don't have the owner present, could be illegal, and more importantly, are displacing residents.
